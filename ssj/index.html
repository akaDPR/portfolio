Our Story

A legacy forged in gold, nestled in the heart of Coimbatore

For 44 years, Sri Swarnambigai Jewellers has been a landmark of trust on Vysial Street. What began as a small family store has grown into a destination for hallmark gold, antique pieces, bridal collections and fine silverware.

Every ornament we craft carries the same promise we made in 1981 — purity you can trust, designs you'll treasure, and value that respects your family.
